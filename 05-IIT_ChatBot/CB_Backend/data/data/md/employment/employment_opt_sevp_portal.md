## Clean Policy Text

### Overview
The Office of Global Services will use Illinois Tech’s ISSS Portal (<https://isss.iit.edu/>) for employment reporting and all I-20 requests. Students on OPT and STEM OPT may also use the SEVP Portal to report required information into SEVIS and confirm employment information.

### SEVP Portal Purpose
The SEVP Portal is a tool that allows F-1 students on Post-completion OPT and the STEM OPT extension to submit required reporting directly into SEVIS.

### Account Invitation and Login
When initial OPT begins and the EAD card start date has passed, SEVP sends an email invitation to open an SEVP Portal account. This email invitation is sent from: **do-not-reply.SEVP@ice.dhs.gov**.

- If a student has already created an SEVP Portal account previously, they do not need to create another account.
- If a student has not created an account yet, they should check their spam folder for the invitation email.
- If the invitation email is missing or a password reset link is needed, students may contact OGS via email at: **global@illinoistech.edu**.

### STEM OPT Reporting (Important)
STEM OPT students only: all material changes or new I-983 Training Plans must be uploaded within the **“OPT/STEM OPT Employment Reporting”** request in the ISSS Portal for reporting purposes.
