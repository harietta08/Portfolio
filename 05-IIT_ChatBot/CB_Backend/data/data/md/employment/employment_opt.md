- OPT is a USCIS-authorized work benefit for F-1 students: up to **12 months**, with possible **24-month STEM extension**.
- **Canvas OPT Tutorial is mandatory** before OGS will accept an OPT application.
- OPT I-20 request flow: **Canvas tutorial → quiz + screenshot → pay $100 fee → ISSS Portal “Initial OPT Request (12 Months)” → advisor confirmation**.
- OPT workshops are listed (Spring 2026 virtual dates) and a prior recording link is provided.
- “When to Apply” includes **earliest** and **latest** filing windows (USCIS lockbox receipt deadline).
- Premium Processing (Form I-907) is optional; listed as **30 business days** target; fee shown as **$1,780** (effective Mar 1, 2026).
- USCIS filing fee update (effective Apr 1, 2024): **$470 online** / **$520 paper** for Form I-765 (OPT/STEM OPT).

- Processing delays can occur; track with USCIS receipt number; students must not work until eligible per EAD rules.

## Rules / Requirements
### Eligibility (Definition)
- OPT is practical training in the student’s **field of study**, authorized by **USCIS**.
- Standard OPT duration: up to **12 months** (STEM extension can add **24 months** if eligible).

### Mandatory Pre-Step (Before OGS accepts OPT)
- Complete the **OPT Tutorial in Canvas**.
- Complete the quiz and save the **OPT Tutorial Quiz Completion Screenshot**.

### OPT I-20 Request (ISSS Portal)
- Submit **“Initial OPT Request (12 Months)”** in **ISSS Portal**.
- Pay **$100 OPT administrative fee** inside the request.
- Provide **Academic Advisor** details for electronic confirmation.

## Clean Policy Text

### OPT Overview
Optional Practical Training (OPT) is for undergraduate and graduate students with F-1 status who have completed or have been pursuing their degrees and are permitted by USCIS to work for at most 12 months, or up to an additional 24 months with an extension, for practical training in their field of study.

### Required OPT Tutorial (Canvas)
All students must complete the OPT tutorial through Canvas before OGS will accept an OPT application. To begin the application process, review the OGS OPT Tutorial in Canvas.

### OPT I-20 Application Process
1. Review the OGS OPT Tutorial on Canvas (self-enroll using the OPT tutorial instructions).
2. Complete the OPT Tutorial quiz and save the “OPT Tutorial Quiz Completion Screenshot”.
3. Pay the $100 OPT administrative fee within the ISSS request.
4. Submit an “Initial OPT Request (12 Months)” in the ISSS Portal and provide Academic Advisor details for electronic confirmation.
