## Key Points (5–10 bullets)
- To apply for the STEM OPT extension, you first request a **STEM OPT I-20** from IIT via the **ISSS Portal**.
- The ISSS Portal request requires a **completed Form I-983**, EAD copy (front/back if needed), and a **$200 STEM OPT administrative fee**.
- OGS processing time for the new STEM OPT I-20 is listed as **~7 business days**.
- After the I-20 is ready in the ISSS Portal, OGS sends an email with USCIS filing instructions.
- USCIS must receive the STEM OPT application **within 60 days of the STEM OPT I-20 issue date** and **before the current EAD expires**.
- USCIS filing methods: **USCIS online account** OR **mail to USCIS lockbox**.
- Online vs mail filing has slightly different checklist items (photos, fee amount, additional forms like G-1145/G-1450).
- IIT/OGS instructions are guidance only; **USCIS is the adjudicator**, and the applicant is responsible for accuracy/completeness.

## Rules / Requirements

### STEM OPT I-20 Request (IIT ISSS Portal)
- Submit a **“STEM OPT Extension (24 Months)”** request in the **ISSS Portal** with:
  - **Completed Form I-983**
  - **Front and back copy of OPT EAD card** (if not previously submitted)
  - **Payment of the $200 STEM OPT Administrative Fee** (inside the ISSS request)
- OGS processing time: **~7 business days** for the STEM OPT I-20.

### USCIS Filing Timing Requirement (Hard Deadline Rule)
- USCIS must receive the STEM OPT application:
  - **within 60 days of the STEM OPT I-20 issue date**, AND
  - **before the current EAD card expires**

### USCIS Filing Options
- File via:
  - **USCIS Online Account**, OR
  - **Mail to USCIS Lockbox** (addresses depend on delivery method)

## Clean Policy Text

### STEM OPT I-20 Process (ISSS Portal)
Submit a “STEM OPT Extension (24 Months)” request within the ISSS Portal with:
- Completed Form I-983
- Front and back copy of the OPT EAD card (if not previously submitted)
- Payment of the $200 STEM OPT Administrative Fee within the ISSS request

OGS advisers review the request and process a new STEM OPT I-20 in approximately 7 business days.
Once the I-20 is ready to download in the ISSS Portal, OGS sends an email with instructions on how to apply to USCIS.

### USCIS Submission Requirement (Timing)
It is the student’s responsibility to ensure USCIS receives the required documents within 60 days of the STEM OPT I-20 issue date and before the expiration of the current EAD card.

### USCIS Filing Methods
There are two ways to file the STEM OPT application with USCIS:
1. Filing via USCIS Online Account
2. Filing by mail to the USCIS Lockbox Address

> Note: These directions are meant to assist in completing the application and should not be considered legal advice.
> USCIS is the adjudicator. The applicant is responsible for ensuring the application is accurate and complete.
> Incomplete applications may cause delays or denial.

### Filing via USCIS Online Account
Online filing allows applicants to submit forms electronically, check case status, and receive notices online.

#### Step 1: Gather and Scan Supporting Documents
- Photocopy of STEM OPT I-20 endorsed by the DSO within 60 days before filing Form I-765
- Copy of most recent I-94 record
- Employment Authorization Document OR Government ID:
  - Previously issued EADs, including current EAD card
- Photocopy of passport biographic page and expiration date (if on another page)
- Photocopy of U.S. F-1 visa stamp
- Filing fee for $470 payable to U.S. Department of Homeland Security (paid online by credit card/debit card/bank account)
- One recent U.S. passport-style photograph (photo tool referenced below)
- Photocopy of IIT transcript or diploma stating the STEM degree
- Proof of institution accreditation ONLY if STEM extension is based on a previously earned STEM degree
- If applicable: copy of I-797 (F-1 approval notice) if immigration status changed while in the U.S.

#### Step 2: Create a USCIS Account / Sign In
#### Step 3: Select the I-765 Form to File
#### Step 4: Complete the I-765 (use USCIS instructions)
#### Step 5: Review and Submit

### Filing by Mail to USCIS Lockbox
#### Step 1: Gather Supporting Documents
- Completed Form I-765 (keep a copy)
- Photocopy of STEM OPT I-20 endorsed by the DSO within 60 days before filing Form I-765
- Photocopies of all previously issued I-20s for current degree level (sign I-20s before photocopying)
- Copy of most recent I-94 record
- Photocopy of passport biographic page and expiration date (if on another page)
- Photocopy of U.S. F-1 visa stamp
- Filing fee for $520 payable to U.S. Department of Homeland Security
  - Pay by money order/personal check/cashier’s check, or credit card using Form G-1450
- Two recent U.S. passport-style photographs
  - Write name and I-94 number on the back of each photo (pencil)
- Photocopy (front and back) of previous EAD cards
- Photocopy of IIT transcript or diploma stating STEM degree
- Completed Form G-1145 (E-Notification of Application/Petition Acceptance)
- If applicable: copy of I-797 (F-1 approval notice) if immigration status changed while in the U.S.

#### Step 3: Send Application to USCIS

## Administrative Deadlines
- **USCIS receipt deadline:** USCIS must receive the application **within 60 days of STEM OPT I-20 issue date** AND **before current EAD expires**.
