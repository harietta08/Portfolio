# Title: OPT / STEM OPT Reporting Requirements (OGS / ISSS Portal)
Category: employment

## Key Points (5–10 bullets)
- While on post-completion OPT and STEM OPT, students must report required updates to OGS through the ISSS Portal to maintain F-1 status.
- Post-completion OPT employment must be related to the field of study and must total **at least 20 hours/week**; employment can be paid or unpaid.
- STEM OPT employment must be related to the field of study, **at least 20 hours/week**, employer must be **E-Verified**, employment must be **paid**, and Form **I-983** is required.
- Students must submit a copy of the EAD card via ISSS Portal using the **“OPT/STEM OPT EAD Card Submission”** request.
- Students must report specific changes **within 10 days** via ISSS Portal (address, employment/unemployment, name, status change, final departure).
- STEM OPT students must submit a **STEM OPT Validation Report every 6 months** (6/12/18/24 months), even if no changes.
- STEM OPT students must submit Form I-983 self-evaluations at **12 months**, **24 months**, and **end of employment** via the STEM OPT Validation Report request.
- If there are material changes to the training plan or a change of employer, a **new Form I-983** must be submitted via the STEM OPT Validation Report request.

## Rules / Requirements

### Employer Eligibility – Post-Completion OPT
- Employment must be related to the field of study.
- Must work at least **20 hours/week**.
- Employment can be **paid or unpaid**.

### Student Reporting – Post-Completion OPT (ISSS Portal)
- Submit EAD card copy via: **“OPT/STEM OPT EAD Card Submission”** request.
- Within **10 days** of a change, submit the applicable request(s):
  - **Contact Information Update** (U.S. home address + permanent foreign address)
  - **OPT/STEM OPT Employment Reporting** (new employment, change of employer, unemployment)
  - **Update F-1 Biographical Information** (legal name change)
  - **Change in Immigration Status**
  - **Permanent/Final Departure from U.S.**

### Employer Eligibility – STEM OPT Extension
- Employment must be related to the field of study.
- Must work at least **20 hours/week**.
- Employer must be **E-Verified**.
- Employment must be **paid**.
- **Form I-983** training plan must be completed.
- Must be a bona fide employee of the employer completing the Form I-983.

### Student Reporting – STEM OPT Extension (ISSS Portal)
- Submit EAD card copy via: **“OPT/STEM OPT EAD Card Submission”** request.
- Within **10 days** of a change, submit the applicable request(s):
  - **Contact Information Update** (U.S. home address + permanent foreign address)
  - **OPT/STEM OPT Employment Reporting** (new employment, change of employer, unemployment)
  - **Update F-1 Biographical Information** (legal name change)
  - **Change in Immigration Status**
  - **Permanent/Final Departure from U.S.**
- Submit **“STEM OPT Validation Report”** every **6 months** after STEM OPT begins (even if no changes):
  - 6-month, 12-month, 18-month, 24-month validation
- Submit Form I-983 self-evaluations within the **“STEM OPT Validation Report”** request:
  - **Evaluation of Student Progress** (Form I-983 Page 5) at **12 months**
  - **Final Evaluation of Student Progress** (Form I-983 Page 5) at **24 months** and **end of any STEM OPT employment**
- Submit a **new Form I-983** if there are **material changes** to the training plan or if **changing employers** (within “STEM OPT Validation Report”).

## Clean Policy Text

### Overview
While on post-completion OPT and STEM OPT periods, students are required to make reports to the Office of Global Services (OGS) as part of maintaining F-1 status. Following the reporting requirements is required to avoid falling out of status.

### Employer Eligibility Requirements – Post-Completion OPT
- Employment must be related to the field of study.
- Must work at least 20 hours a week.
- Employment can be paid or unpaid.

### Student Reporting Requirements – Post-Completion OPT
Students are required to submit a copy of the Employment Authorization Document (EAD) by submitting an **“OPT/STEM OPT EAD Card Submission”** request to OGS within the **ISSS Portal**.

Students are required to submit the following requests within **10 days** of the change to the **ISSS Portal**:
- Contact Information Update for U.S. Home Address and Permanent Foreign Address
- OPT/STEM OPT Employment Reporting for New Employment, Change of Employer, and Unemployment
- Update F-1 Biographical Information for Legal Name Changes
- Change in Immigration Status
- Permanent/Final Departure from U.S.

### Employer Eligibility Requirements – STEM OPT Extension
- Employment must be related to field of study.
- Must work at least 20 hours a week.
- Employer must be e-verified.
- Employment must be paid.
- A completed Form I-983 training plan must be completed.
- Must be a bona fide employee of the employer completing the Form I-983 training plan.

### Student Reporting Requirements – STEM OPT Extension
Students are required to submit a copy of the Employment Authorization Document (EAD) by submitting an **“OPT/STEM OPT EAD Card Submission”** request to OGS within the **ISSS Portal**.

Students are required to submit the following requests within **10 days** of the change to the **ISSS Portal**:
- Contact Information Update for U.S. Home Address and Permanent Foreign Address
- OPT/STEM OPT Employment Reporting for New Employment, Change of Employer, and Unemployment
- Update F-1 Biographical Information for Legal Name Changes
- Change in Immigration Status
- Permanent/Final Departure from U.S.

Students are required to submit **“STEM OPT Validation Report”** requests in the **ISSS Portal** every **six months** after the beginning of the STEM OPT period (even if no changes have occurred):
- Six-month validation
- 12-month validation
- 18-month validation
- 24-month validation

Students are required to submit the self-evaluations from **Form I-983** every 12 months after the beginning of STEM OPT and at the end of any employment, in addition to the 12- and 24-month deadlines. Evaluations are submitted within the **“STEM OPT Validation Report”** requests to the ISSS Portal:
- Evaluation of Student Progress — Form I-983, Page 5 (12 months after the beginning of STEM OPT)
- Final Evaluation of Student Progress — Form I-983, Page 5 (24 months after the beginning of STEM OPT and at the end of any STEM OPT employment)

A new Form I-983 must be resubmitted if there are any material changes to the training plan under the current employer or if the student changes employers. New Forms I-983 should be submitted within the **“STEM OPT Validation Report”** requests to the ISSS Portal.

## Administrative Deadlines
- **Within 10 days**: report address changes, employment/unemployment updates, name changes, immigration status changes, and final departure via ISSS Portal.
- **Every 6 months (STEM OPT)**: submit **STEM OPT Validation Report** at 6/12/18/24 months (even if no changes).
- **12 months (STEM OPT)**: submit I-983 **Evaluation of Student Progress** (Page 5) via Validation Report.
- **24 months (STEM OPT)**: submit I-983 **Final Evaluation of Student Progress** (Page 5) via Validation Report.
- **End of any STEM OPT employment**: submit I-983 **Final Evaluation** via Validation Report.
