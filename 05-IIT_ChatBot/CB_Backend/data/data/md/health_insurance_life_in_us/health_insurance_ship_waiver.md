## Clean Policy Text

### What You Need to Know
Health insurance choice does not affect services received at the Student Health and Wellness Center.

All students will be added to the Illinois Tech Student Health Insurance Plan once enrolled in one academic credit hour of class. Accounts will be billed according to the term of entry.

All Illinois Tech students are required to show proof of health insurance that complies with U.S. Department of State guidelines. Illinois Tech requires all international students to purchase Illinois Tech’s health insurance provided through Aetna Student Health. Waivers for comparable health insurance plans may apply.

### Waiving SHIP (If Covered Under Another Plan)
Students covered under a different plan must waive SHIP enrollment during the open-enrollment period before the stated deadline (September 1). To waive enrollment, students must affirm active alternate insurance that meets all comparable coverage requirements.

### Coverage Dates and Insurance Card Access
Coverage begins August 10, 2025 (fall) or January 6, 2026 (spring) and continues through August 9, 2026.

Health insurance cards and documents will not be mailed out by default. Students can log on to the Aetna Student Health website and use the “Print Your Insurance Card” feature, or contact Aetna to request a mailed card.

### Comparable Coverage Checklist (Waiver Eligibility)
Not all forms of health insurance are accepted to waive the student plan. Students are not permitted to waive SHIP if they have coverage through a travel insurance company, a company outside of the U.S., a company that only provides monthly coverage, or a life insurance company.

Students must be able to answer “YES” to all comparable coverage checklist items, including activation by August 1, 2025, local in-network access in Chicago for routine and emergency care (including mental health), deductible limits, U.S.-approved ACA-compliant carrier requirements, coverage of pre-existing conditions with no waiting period, unlimited policy-year maximum, and coverage for injuries/illness due to alcohol, drugs, or attempted suicide.

### Are You Graduating / Fall-Only Coverage
Students graduating or needing coverage only for fall may complete the “fall only” form by or before December 12, 2025. If enrolled in the fall-only option, coverage is valid from August 9, 2025 to January 5, 2026. The cost is listed as $TBD. Students must be logged into the Illinois Tech Portal to access the fall-only form.
