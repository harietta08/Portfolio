## Key Points (5–10 bullets)
- Illinois Tech assesses multiple mandatory and optional student fees tied to campus services, enrollment status, and administrative processes.
- Some fees apply to all students; others apply only to specific student groups (e.g., Stuart students, graduating graduate students).
- Student Health Insurance is assessed to domestic students unless waived and is mandatory for international students in F-1/J-1 status when enrolled in at least one billable hour.
- U-Pass is optional effective Fall 2023 and eligibility includes part-time students registered for 6 credit hours or more.
- Late fees may be assessed monthly on delinquent accounts and for late payment plan installments.
- Some fees apply only if a student opts in (e.g., UMII Scholarship Fee, U-Pass).
- Tuition and fees may be revised by Illinois Tech’s Board of Trustees.

## Rules / Requirements
- Student Health Insurance:
  - Assessed to domestic students enrolled in at least one billable hour if adequate proof of insurance is not provided by the waiver deadline.
  - Mandatory for all international students on an F-1 or J-1 visa for every term enrolled in at least one billable hour.
- U-Pass:
  - Optional enrollment continues indefinitely effective Fall 2023.
  - Eligibility expanded to part-time students registered for 6 credit hours or more per semester.
  - Undergraduate and graduate students may opt-in after registered for 6 billable academic hours.
  - U-Pass entitlements are not valid between academic terms.
- Graduate Continuation Studies Fee:
  - Graduate students must register for a course during the semester of graduation.
  - Failure results in administrative registration and an automatic charge that will not be waived.
- Late Fees:
  - Assessed to delinquent accounts at 2%–5% per month.
  - Payment plan late payments may incur a monthly late fee.
- Tuition and fees are subject to revision by Illinois Tech’s Board of Trustees.

## Clean Policy Text

### Activity Fee
A mandatory fee supporting campus activities and student organizations. Allocations are handled by students via the Finance Board with accountability to the Student Government Association. Supports operations, programming, conferences, and large campus events.

### Co-Curricular Fee
A mandatory fee assessed to all IIT Stuart students in fall and spring. Supports co-curricular programs that enhance academic experience and career readiness.

### Graduate Continuation Studies Fee
Graduate students must register for a course during the semester of graduation. Failure results in administrative registration and automatic charge of the graduate continuing student course fee before degree conferral. The fee will not be waived.

### Graduation Fee
A mandatory fee covering administrative costs related to graduation application, checkout, posting, and degree reporting. Late graduation applications may be assessed a late graduation fee.

### New Student Fee
A mandatory one-time fee for all new students. Covers orientation at the beginning of each semester and funds first-year student programs.

### Student Health Insurance
Health insurance is assessed to all domestic students enrolled in at least one billable hour who have not provided adequate proof of insurance to Student Health Services by the waiver deadline. Health insurance is mandatory for all international students on an F-1 or J-1 visa for every term the student is enrolled in at least one billable hour.

### Student Service Fee
A mandatory fee supporting departments such as Athletics, Paul V. Galvin Library, Career Services, Technology Services, and the Student Health and Wellness Center.

### U-Pass Fee
Illinois Tech participates in CTA’s U-Pass program for reduced-cost transportation with unlimited CTA rides during an academic term. Ventra cards are issued to participating students and intended to last through enrollment.

U-Pass entitlements are not valid between academic terms. Students must add transit value for rides between terms. Additional rides taken for others on the same trip may be charged as full fare and can cause negative balance that must be repaid.

Effective Fall 2023, optional U-Pass enrollment continues indefinitely. Eligibility includes part-time students registered for 6 credit hours or more per semester. Undergraduate and graduate students may opt in after registering for 6 billable academic hours.

### Deferment Plan Fee
Assessed to students who enroll in IIT’s tuition deferment program during open registration dates.

### Late Fee
Assessed to delinquent student accounts at 2%–5% per month. Students in a payment plan who pay after the due date are assessed a monthly late fee.

### Late Registration Fee
Students should register early; late registration may require paying a late registration fee. Registration periods and late registration information are available in the Course Schedule.

### Payment Plan Fee
Assessed to students who enroll in a formal payment plan during open registration dates.

### Returned Check Fee
Assessed each time a check or e-check is returned unpaid due to insufficient funds, invalid info, stop payment, or other reasons.

### UMII Scholarship Fee (Opt-In)
An opt-in fee supporting scholarships for undergraduate undocumented students ineligible for federal financial aid (FAFSA). Donations are presented during payments through Manage My Account and are tax-deductible.

### Revision Notice
All tuition and fees are subject to revision by Illinois Tech’s Board of Trustees.
