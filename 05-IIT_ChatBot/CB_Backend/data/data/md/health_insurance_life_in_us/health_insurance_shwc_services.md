## Clean Policy Text

### Welcome / Purpose
The Student Health and Wellness Center supports overall student well-being through services that promote mental health, personal growth, and a thriving campus community.

### Services

#### Counseling & Mental Health Support
- Individual counseling sessions with licensed therapists and supervised interns
- Crisis triage and same-day support for urgent emotional concerns
- Referrals to trusted off-campus providers for specialized care

#### Administrative & Student Support
- Assistance with student health insurance enrollment, waivers, and benefits
- Immunization documentation consultation and compliance tracking
- Guidance for connecting with community-based health resources

### Virtual & After-Hours Support (Illinois Tech Care Hub)
Students enrolled in the Aetna Student Health Insurance Plan have free access to Academic Live Care (Care Hub), a 24/7 virtual health and wellness platform that offers mental health and medical care anywhere in the U.S. Students can call SHWC or email to receive a passcode.

#### Mental Health Services via Care Hub
- Connect with licensed counselors for ongoing therapy or same-day appointments
- On-demand emotional support lines for non-crisis concerns
- Year-round availability, including breaks and off-campus periods

#### Medical Services via Care Hub
- On-demand virtual visits with board-certified providers
- Treatment for common illnesses (cold, flu, sinus infections, pink eye, skin rashes)
- Sexual and reproductive health consultations (birth control and STI guidance)
- Care coordination and follow-up instructions when in-person evaluation is recommended

### FAQ

#### Where can students get missing vaccines?
Vaccines are available at most walk-in or take-care clinics. Student health insurance may help cover costs. Students should bring their insurance card.

Closest facilities listed:
- Mies Campus: Walgreens, 3000 S Halsted St, Chicago, IL 60608
- Conviser Law Center: CVS Pharmacy, 400 W. Madison St, Chicago, IL 60606

Additional facilities can be found at the Aetna Student Health site using the “docfind” feature.

#### Where can students get their Aetna insurance card?
Students can access their Aetna Student Health Insurance card online using the provided link.
