## Clean Policy Text

### Overview
Students are encouraged to plan ahead and apply as early as possible for an updated I-20 or DS-2019 as needed. Late or rushed requests cannot be honored.

To avoid delays, students must complete all required information on the request form and submit all required supporting documentation, such as financial documentation when requesting a program extension or adding a dependent.

Students will receive an email once the request is ready for download on the ISSS Portal.

OGS reserves the right to adjust processing times as needed; students should plan ahead and apply as early as possible for updated documents.
