## Clean Policy Text

### Overview
Institutions approved to issue I-20 or DS-2019 are required to maintain specific documents and information on all F-1 and J-1 students and their dependents. To comply with these record-keeping requirements, Illinois Institute of Technology’s Office of Global Services (OGS) requires all incoming new F-1 and J-1 students to complete a check-in after arrival.

### Check-In Hold
Incoming new F-1 and J-1 students have a registration hold placed on their student account until check-in is completed with OGS. This hold does not prevent enrolling in courses or obtaining a student ID card. The OGS check-in is separate from SOAR (orientation) and housing check-ins.

### Spring 2026 Check-In (Virtual)
All incoming international students, including transfer-in international students, are required to check in with OGS shortly after arriving in the United States. Check-in takes place virtually through the ISSS Portal. Students log in using Illinois Tech (Okta Access) credentials and submit a “New Student Check-In” request.

### Required Documents / Information for Check-In
- Visa
- I-94 arrival record
- Local U.S. address
- Local telephone number
- Emergency contact information

### Processing and Completion
OGS processes the request within 2 business days and removes the International Check-In Hold on the student account. Students only need to check in once with OGS.

### Support
OGS is open Monday–Friday from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Students may email OGS with questions.
