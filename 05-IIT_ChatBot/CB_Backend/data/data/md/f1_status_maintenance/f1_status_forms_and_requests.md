## Key Points (5–10 bullets)
- All F-1 and J-1 students use the ISSS Portal as the primary location to submit I-20 and DS-2019 requests to OGS.
- Requests submitted through the ISSS Portal are processed electronically by OGS.
- Completed electronic I-20 and DS-2019 forms (PDF) are available for download directly in the ISSS Portal.
- The ISSS Portal supports requests related to maintaining status, employment, transfers, dependents, and program updates.
- Some requests require additional department-specific or program-specific forms.
- Separate request groupings exist for F-1 students, J-1 students, and J-1 scholars.
- Portal navigation demos are available for both students and scholars.

## Rules / Requirements
- Requests related to I-20 and DS-2019 processing must be submitted through the ISSS Portal.
- Students/scholars must use the correct request type based on visa category and purpose.
- Supporting documents and additional forms may be required depending on the request.
- Electronic I-20/DS-2019 forms must be downloaded from the portal after completion.

## Clean Policy Text

### Overview
All F-1 and J-1 students are able to use the ISSS Portal as the one-stop location to submit all I-20 and DS-2019 requests to OGS. The electronic I-20 and DS-2019 (PDF) will be available for students to download directly in the portal once their request has been completed.

Please refer below for the specific names of requests possible to submit in the ISSS Portal and any valid forms still used by the Office of Global Services.

### Maintaining Status

#### F-1 Maintaining Status Requests
- Change of Degree Level
- Change of Status
- Create I-20 (for new students)
- F-1 Change of Academic Program
- F-1 Change of Personal Information
- F-1 Program Extension
- F-1 Reduced Course Load
- F-1 SEVIS Transfer In (for new students)
- F-1 SEVIS Transfer Out
- Leave of Absence/Withdrawal
- F-2 Dependent Request
- Reprint I-20

#### J-1 Maintaining Status Requests
- J-1 Change of Academic Program
- J-1 Change of Personal Information
- J-1 Program Extension
- J-1 Reduced Course Load
- J-1 SEVIS Transfer Out
- Leave of Absence/Withdrawal
- J-2 Dependent Request
- Reprint DS-2019 for J-1

### Employment

#### F-1 Employment Requests
- Cap Gap Extension
- Curricular Practical Training (CPT)
- Initial OPT Request (12 Months)
- OPT/STEM OPT EAD Card Submission
- OPT/STEM OPT Employment Reporting
- STEM OPT Extension (24 Months)
- STEM OPT Validation Report
- Severe Economic Hardship

#### J-1 Employment Requests
- Academic Training
- On-Campus Employment Authorization

### Transferring Into Illinois Institute of Technology

#### F-1 Transfer In (Forms/Requests)
- Transfer In request(s) for F-1 students (graduate, undergraduate, Institute of Design, Chicago-Kent)

#### J-1 Transfer In (Forms/Requests)
- J-1 Students Transfer In Form

### J-1 Scholar
The ISSS Portal is the one-stop location for J-1 scholars to submit all DS-2019 requests to OGS.

#### J-1 Scholar Maintaining Status Requests
- Change of Status
- Contact Information Update
- Departure Form
- J-2 Dependent Request
- New Scholar Check-In
- Out of Country
- Permission to Consult/Lecture
- Program Extension
- Reprint DS-2019 for J-1 (includes Travel)
- Transfer Out
