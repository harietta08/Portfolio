## Clean Policy Text

### Overview
Any student making a transfer-out request from Illinois Tech after July 21, 2025, must attend a full semester of classes at Illinois Tech or be subject to a $2,500 pre-enrollment fee. Transfer-out requests are processed after courses begin using a stated timetable. The fee helps cover administrative costs related to advising and immigration document evaluation and processing.

Students who plan to attend another university before arriving in the U.S. should not enter the U.S. with an Illinois Tech I-20 and should obtain an I-20 and updated visa for the university they will attend.

### Transfer Out Processing Dates (Operational Timetable)
OGS prioritizes students intending to study at Illinois Tech and sets operational processes for transfer-outs. Students seeking to transfer out without attending Illinois Tech will be served according to the timetable described.

### Transfer Out Timing Requirements (Legal Constraint + Options)
DHS regulations require F-1 students to be enrolled in courses within 30 days of first arrival in the U.S. Before arranging a transfer, the new university start date must be within 30 days of U.S. arrival.

If the new start date is within 30 days, the student may proceed with the transfer if Illinois Tech transfer requirements are met, including payment of the $2,500 fee (if applicable).

If not within 30 days, options include:
- Option A: Enroll at Illinois Tech for at least one semester, then transfer out.
- Option B: Leave the U.S. and return later to attend the desired university (requires authorized early withdrawal, a new I-20 from the desired university, and paying the SEVIS I-901 fee again).
- Option C: Proceed with transfer by terminating the SEVIS record and pursuing reinstatement to remain legally in the U.S. (not all universities accept terminated records; confirm with the new school).

### Transfer Out Document Requirements
OGS begins transferring SEVIS records after the start of courses if all requirements are complete, including proof of admission, fee payment (if applicable), U.S. address, visa copy, signed I-20 copy, and I-94 documentation.

### Transferring Your SEVIS Record
The SEVIS record must reflect the current academic institution. Transfer is required when beginning a new program at a new university after graduating, or when starting at a new institution without completing studies at Illinois Tech.

### Important Notes About Transferring Your Record
A SEVIS transfer transfers immigration status, not academic credits. Once transferred, employment must stop (including on-campus work and CPT/OPT/AT). Travel may impact the transfer release date and should be discussed with OGS. After transfer, OGS no longer has access and the transfer cannot be canceled or reversed.

### Eligibility
Students must be maintaining F-1/J-1 status at Illinois Tech. Eligibility includes full-time enrollment, OPT/STEM OPT/Academic Training, successful OGS check-in for new arrivals, and recent graduates within stated grace periods. J-1 transfers have additional restrictions and should be discussed with OGS.

### Procedures
Students submit an “F-1 SEVIS Transfer Out” or “J-1 SEVIS Transfer Out” request in the ISSS Portal with:
- Admission letter from another U.S. institution
- New school’s transfer form (if applicable)

Processing time is two business days; submit a complete request at least two business days prior to the desired transfer date.

Current Illinois Tech students must withdraw their academic record online through the Illinois Tech Portal. Students must report to the international office at the new school to receive a “Transfer Pending” I-20 with the same SEVIS ID or a transfer DS-2019.
